What is this product?

One of the major issues we face in the current scenario is various forms of pollution around us, and one among which is the noise pollution. For instance, in a metropolitan city you have all forms of transport- air, land and rail together, and the amount of noise pollution is something huge that we cannot even imagine. Survival of humans is impossible without these technological advancements, but at the same time this much of pollution also leads us to harmful effects in the environment. This arises the need for reduction in noise pollution to the best possible extent, and for which we need to first measure the amount of noise pollution in the surroundings. Our aim of this product is to design a Noise Pollution Measurement System to monitor the levels of noise in the surroundings at constant intervals in the day, which is efficient and cost effective. Further the measured values need to be published in a website using internet of things technology.

The market for this product is majorly the government agencies, who can procure this for monitoring noise pollution and thereby looking on measures to reduce it. This product can be used in crowded places, where constant monitoring of noise is needed. This product can also be used in industries, where additional care is required in maintaining the noise level.
